Galatic Space Duel

In this epic space battle, you'll control your paddle using the arrow keys:

Player 1 (Left Paddle):

  • Move up: W key
  • Move down: S key

Player 2 (Right Paddle):

  • Move up: Arrow Up key
  • Move down: Arrow Down key

Scoring:

  • When the ball passes the opponent's paddle, the opposing player scores a point.
  • The game ends when one player reaches a score of 10.
